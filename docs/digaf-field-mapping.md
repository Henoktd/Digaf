# Digaf Field Mapping - Stage 65

## Purpose

This document maps fields identified during Stage 65 for the Digaf Shareholder Governance Platform production-ready pilot preparation.

## Source Classification

- **Digaf Shareholder Registration Template (Excel, primary Digaf-provided source):** `docs/source-inputs/Digaf_Shareholder_Registration_Template 2.xlsx`
- **Proposed Shareholder KYC Form — Draft for Digaf Review:** `docs/source-inputs/Shareholder_KYC_Form.docx`

The Excel registration template is treated as the primary Digaf source input. The KYC form is AI-generated by the implementation team and is not an approved Digaf policy, official regulatory document, or final KYC standard. Fields sourced only from the draft KYC form are marked as assumed or requiring Digaf validation.

## Status Legend

- **Confirmed:** Present in the Digaf-provided Excel template.
- **Assumed:** Proposed for the pilot based on the draft KYC form or implementation planning.
- **Needs Digaf Validation:** Requires confirmation by Digaf Compliance, management, or authorized business owners before schema, workflow, or form changes.
